## Verdict

**A RETRAVAILLER**

The underlying business case is solid and the financial logic is credible, but the slide has two issues that will cost you in the room: the title buries the ROI (your strongest argument), and the body layout creates a visual tension between the chart and the bullets that a CFO will notice immediately.

---

## 1. Structure du message & logique
**Score: 74/100**

**Ce qui fonctionne:**
- The title is a genuine action title — it states a claim ("18% is achievable by Q4"), not a topic label. Correct McKinsey-style construction.
- The three bullets follow a consistent logic: action → financial outcome → timeline. Parallel structure, respects the 3x3 rule.
- The summary financials (gross / investment / net) form a mini-proof point reinforcing the title claim.

**Problèmes:**
- The title leads with a percentage (relative number) but the CFO's first question is "what is that in euros?" The absolute net benefit — €2.8M on €0.6M investment, a 4.7x ROI — is buried in the body.
- The three actions receive identical visual weight despite radically different financial materiality: carrier renegotiation (€2.1M, 66% of savings) vs. SKU consolidation (€0.4M, 12%).
- "Consolidate SKUs below 2% velocity" uses internal warehouse KPI language, not CFO vocabulary.

**Recommandation prioritaire:**
> Move the net benefit and ROI multiple into the title or subtitle. The CFO must see "€2.8M net benefit at 4.7x ROI" within 3 seconds of looking at the slide.

---

## 2. Qualité du design visuel
**Score: 78/100**

**Ce qui fonctionne:**
- Clean white background with generous whitespace — correct for a CFO audience.
- Dark blue + orange accent is a coherent professional palette; orange as accent is a correct use of pre-attentive attributes.
- Consistent font throughout.
- A horizontal bar chart for a before/after cost comparison is the right chart type.

**Problèmes:**
- Chart on the left, bullets on the right creates a visual split with no resolution. No dominant anchor tells the eye where to go first.
- Equal visual weight across three bullets misrepresents relative importance (€2.1M vs. €0.4M).
- If the bar chart shows only total before/after, it misses the opportunity to disaggregate the three levers visually.

**Recommandation prioritaire:**
> Replace the simple before/after bar with a segmented bar that shows each of the three savings levers as a distinct segment (color-coded, with euro labels).

---

## 3. Impact & lisibilité en 5 secondes
**Score: 71/100**

**Test des 5 secondes :**
*"En lisant uniquement le titre pendant 5 secondes, un dirigeant comprend : that an 18% cost reduction is achievable by Q4 via three actions. But they do NOT understand the financial return, the investment required, or why they should approve this today."*

**Ce qui fonctionne:**
- The title passes the basic action-title test: claim + deadline + implies a path.
- The three savings figures (€2.1M + €0.9M + €0.4M = €3.4M) invite mental verification.

**Problèmes:**
- The title is 14 words. Top firms target 8–12. Beyond that, the chunking mechanism breaks down.
- "3 targeted actions" adds zero information — every recommended action is, by definition, targeted.
- The investment required (€0.6M, the CFO's gating variable) is buried in the body.

**Recommandation prioritaire:**
> "Three warehouse actions deliver €2.8M net savings by Q4" — 9 words, CFO reads the financial outcome in 5 seconds.

## 4. Suggestions de reformulation
**Score actuel du message: 74/100**

**Titre actuel :**
> "Reducing warehouse costs by 18% is achievable by Q4 through 3 targeted actions"

**Titre proposé :**
> "Three warehouse actions deliver €2.8M net savings by Q4 — at 4.7x ROI"

**Corps du message — reformulation suggérée :**

| Action | Savings | Investment | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|
| Renegotiate carrier contracts | €2.1M | Minimal | 6 weeks |
| Automate receiving dock (Lyon pilot) | €0.9M | €0.5M capex | 3 months |
| Rationalize tail-end SKUs (<2% velocity) | €0.4M | — | Immediate |
| **Total** | **€3.4M** | **€0.6M** | **By Q4** |

**Éléments à supprimer complètement :**
- "targeted actions" in the title — noise, not signal
- The standalone financial summary sentence — belongs as a table summary row, not a loose sentence
- Any secondary use of the orange accent — one element only
