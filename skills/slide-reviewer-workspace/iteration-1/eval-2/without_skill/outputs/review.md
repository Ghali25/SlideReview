# Slide Review

**Slide Title:** "Reducing warehouse costs by 18% is achievable by Q4 through 3 targeted actions"

---

## Overall Assessment

This is a strong, well-structured executive slide. It follows the Pyramid Principle with a clear headline claim, supported by three concrete actions, and closes with a compelling financial summary. Ready to send with minor refinements noted below.

---

## Title

**Strength:** The title is specific, quantified (18%), time-bound (Q4), and action-oriented. A CFO will immediately understand what the slide is about and what is being asked of them.

**Consideration:** The phrase "is achievable" introduces a slight hedge. If you are confident in the analysis, consider sharpening to: "Reducing warehouse costs by 18% by Q4 through 3 targeted actions" — removing the qualifier makes the claim more assertive, which is appropriate for a CFO audience that expects a point of view.

---

## Body — Three Action Bullets

**Bullet 1: Renegotiate carrier contracts → saves €2.1M (implement in 6 weeks)**
- Largest savings lever at 62% of total. The CFO will want to know the basis for this number.
- "6 weeks" is specific and credible. Good.

**Bullet 2: Automate receiving dock → saves €0.9M (pilot in Lyon, 3 months)**
- Naming Lyon grounds the pilot in operational reality. Good.

**Bullet 3: Consolidate SKUs below 2% velocity → saves €0.4M (immediate)**
- "Immediate" is compelling but must be defensible.

---

## Financial Summary

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Total savings | €3.4M |
| Investment required | €0.6M |
| Net benefit | €2.8M |

**Critical gap:** The financial summary does not specify the time horizon for savings. Are these annualized? Cumulative through Q4? This must be clarified — it will be the CFO's first question.

---

## Visual Design

- Bar chart appropriate. Ensure the 18% reduction is labeled directly on the chart.
- White background + dark blue + orange: clean and professional.
- Generous whitespace: correct.

---

## Summary Recommendation

The slide is approximately 85% ready. Before sending:

1. **Clarify the savings time horizon** (annualized vs. through Q4).
2. **Add a brief basis note for the carrier contract saving** (€2.1M).

The financial logic is sound, the structure is clear, and the visual approach is appropriate.
